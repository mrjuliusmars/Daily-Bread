# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2025**

**Daily Bread** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Daily Bread (the "App") on iOS devices.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **App Selections**: When you choose which apps to block, we store your app selection preferences locally on your device
- **Onboarding Information**: Your responses to quiz questions and onboarding preferences are stored locally on your device
- **Streak Data**: Information about your reading streak, including current streak, longest streak, total days, and last read date, is stored locally on your device

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We may collect device-specific information such as your iOS version and device model
- **Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used and time spent in the App

### 1.3 Screen Time Permission
- To provide our core functionality, the App requires Screen Time permissions through Apple's Family Controls framework
- We use this permission solely to block selected apps and display Bible verses as described in this App's functionality
- We do not have access to your detailed screen time data, usage statistics, or app usage patterns beyond what is necessary to block selected apps

### 1.4 Subscription Information
- If you subscribe to Daily Bread, your subscription is managed through Apple's App Store and Superwall
- We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not store your payment information
- Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple and Superwall

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
- Block selected apps and display Bible verses as requested
- Track and display your reading streak
- Process and manage your subscription
- Provide customer support
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
- Most of your data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
- This includes your app selections, streak data, and onboarding preferences
- Data stored locally is not transmitted to our servers

### 3.2 App Groups
- We use Apple's App Groups to share data between the main app and extensions (Device Activity Monitor, Shield Configuration)
- This data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally

### 3.3 Data Security
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information
- However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure
- While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Apple Services
- **App Store**: Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store
- **Family Controls Framework**: We use Apple's Family Controls framework to provide app blocking functionality
- **iCloud**: If you use iCloud, some data may be synced across your devices through Apple's services

### 4.2 Superwall
- We use Superwall to manage paywall displays and subscription status
- Superwall may collect certain information necessary for their services
- For more information about Superwall's privacy practices, please visit: https://superwall.com/privacy

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: We may share information with third-party service providers (such as Superwall) who perform services on our behalf
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities
- **Business Transfers**: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Deletion
- You can access and modify your app selections at any time through the App settings
- You can delete your streak data by uninstalling the App
- You can revoke Screen Time permissions at any time through iOS Settings > Screen Time > Family Controls

### 7.2 Subscription Management
- You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID account settings
- Subscription cancellations are processed by Apple

### 7.3 Data Export
- Currently, we do not provide a data export feature
- If you wish to export your data, please contact us at [your email address]

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.
